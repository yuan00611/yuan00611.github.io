Introduction

This is a usability testing for Tencent video app. The purpose of this research is to understand how the target group uses Tencent video app and the pleasant experience or difficulties generated during use to help improve future design.

Process

hello

hello We observed pedestrians and verified some issues by questionnaires. Based on users’ opinions, we designed “Cue Card”, which integrates light and vibration reminders into original bus cards. In addition to prototyping card’s interface, I also created an Arduino version. After user testing, we published a poster in 2016 TAICHI conference.
