About me

My focus is on Human Computer Interaction(HCI) and Information and Communications Technology(ICT).


UX Research/Design, Front-End Development, Game Design, and Data Analysis/Visualization.

Transmitting and manipulating information with innovative technologies is the most exciting thing to me. I enjoyed using different media to produce straightforward and intriguing content to convey information. Also, embracing new technologies is my daily life. I can't help learning and dig into them, like XR, 3D printing, AI, robotic, etc.

Listening is another superpower of mine. My curiosity and empathy for humans drive me to understand their behaviors, contexts, thoughts, and stories. The more I listen, the more I hope to be the bridge between people and technology.

I am passionate about education, the environment, sustainability, healthcare, and accessibility. I hope my love of the world and people can bring everyone a better life.
